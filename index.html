About Me
Hello! My name is Saodat, and I'm a third-year student studying linguistics at Nazarbayev University. I graduated with honors from Nazarbayev Intellectual School, where I developed a passion for language acquisition and linguistics.
I've been named to the Dean's List three times in three years, which is a testament to my academic achievements and dedication to learning. I've also worked as a research assistant at MultiCorSKL, where I collected and annotated linguistic data.
My ultimate goal is to apply my passion for linguistics and language education to a fulfilling career in the industry. I'm excited about the future and look forward to connecting with like-minded individuals who share my interests and goals.
My interests
My interests include linguistics, French, Kazakh, cooking, philosophy, and feminism. Our project aims to increase the availability of Kazakh language learning materials through film and TV shows.
